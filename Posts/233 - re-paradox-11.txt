The ideal shoe material would be Copper-Nickel plate. Nigel Warren in "Metal Corrosion in Boats" talks about tropical shrimp boat hulls built of Copper-Nickel plate with all the antifouling of copper. Strength would be less than steel and but much higher than copper. Material cost is very high.
