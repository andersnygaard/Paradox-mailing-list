I wouldn't recommend doing an ocean crossing in a Paradox, because there are far better boats suited to the task, but I believe it could be done in a Paradox.

In fact, in my Appraisal of a Paradox at 

http://bills-log.blogspot.com/2010/12/appraisal-of-faith-my-paradox-sailboat.html

I mentioned that I thought she would be capable of doing it.

With the right person doing the right things by sailing the right route at the right time of year, she should make it for her crew to tell the tale.

A foolproof self-steering system would be a great asset.

Even an empty beer barrel can cross an ocean being propelled by winds and currents. No doubt an empty Paradox would do the same.
