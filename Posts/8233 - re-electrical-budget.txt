Read John Letcher's book on self steering. It contains no vagueness yet is not at all heavy on the arcane formulas, and explains methods to methodically figure out how to self-steer with sheet to tiller gear, if it is possible for a given boat. (and the author reckons it is possible for almost all boats.)
